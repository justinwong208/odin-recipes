Smashed Potato

Description

Crispy on the outside, soft and fluffy on the inside. Perfect bites of potato to complement any main as the ultimate side dish.

Ingredients

  • Baby Potatos (Skin on)
  • Olive oil
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Garlic (Can be substituted with Garlic Powder)
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Boil clean potatos for 15 minutes or until soft.
  2. Preheat oven to 400ËšF. Mix together the oil, seasonings, garlic and melted into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Drain and let sit for 10 minutes to dry
  4. On a lined baking sheet, smash the potato into 1/2 inch disks using a cup or bowl
  5. Drizzle the sauce all over the potatos before putting into the oven for 35 minutes (Do not flip!)
